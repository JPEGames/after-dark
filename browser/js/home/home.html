Welcome home, Teslanaut

After Dark is an augmented reality game taking place 500 years after Elon Musk and the Teslanauts left Earth.

You are among the first Teslanauts to return to Earth, now a barren, unforgiving planet. With what remains of your spaceship on arrival, you build a bunker.

Surviving here won't be easy. You will need to find the resources to survive. So you take a gamble and wander outside...
