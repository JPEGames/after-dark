Character Creation


Miner

You are a former miner from Tesla Enterprises, born and bred in the Martian shadows. While not the brightest of mind, you have a knack for extracting valuable Elonium from scrap metal.


  • Increased detection of scrap metal on map (+0.5 km radius)
  • Enhanced processing of collected scrap metal to Elonium (+10%)
 Scientist

You are one of the last surviving scientists from Tesla's think-tank, Minerva. Your brilliance allows you great insight into the latest technologies, significantly boosting the quality of your research. Naturally, you are also a hacker extraordinaire, having been an original member of the top-secret Bunker-Tec program.


  • Increased research output (+15%)
  • Additional clues while hacking other bunkers
 Soldier

Once a distinguished captain in MUSC (Mars United Security Conglomerate), Elon Musk's private task force for maintaining order on the Martian colony, your fall from grace began with a disagreement with a Minerva researcher and ended in exile to Earth. Ever since, you have directed your efforts towards re-building your reputation and finding that one irksome scientist. Your military background makes you particularly skilled at detecting the locations of other survivors and executing strategic strikes to further your goals.


  • Heightened detection radius for other bunkers (+0.5km)
  • Boost to physical attack capabilities
